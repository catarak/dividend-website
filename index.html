Dividend

Dividend is a new digital currency with the goal of empowering communities.

While cryptocurrencies, such as Bitcoin, are by design philosophically neutral, most choose to implement a system of capitalism. Dividend is inspired by ideas of socialism, exploring the ways in which people can own a share of a piece of art. To be a part of this community, you can opt in, and get your share.

Dividend is built on top of the Bitcoin blockchain, and is implemented using a project called Counterparty.

You need a wallet to hold your Dividend coins, just like you use a wallet in real life to hold your physical money.

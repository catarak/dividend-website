Dividend

Dividend is a new digital currency which aims to empower the people.

While cryptocurrencies, such as Bitcoin, are designed to be philosophically neutral, most are traded system of capitalism. Dividend is inspired by ideas of socialism, exploring the ways in which people can own a share of a piece of art. You can opt into this community, and get your share.

Dividend is built on top of the Bitcoin blockchain, and is implemented using a project called Counterparty.

Get a Wallet

You need a wallet to hold your Dividend coins, just like you use a wallet in real life to hold your physical money.

You can download IndieSquare, a mobile wallet for iOS and Android, in the App Store or from Google Play.

You can also create a web wallet using Counterwallet.

For more information about wallets, you can check the Counterparty website.
